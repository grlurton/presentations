Heritages in HIS

In limited resource countries, top down HIS have been designed and implemented in two successive waves :
  • Colonial State : statistics as a tool of political and administrative control and justification
"Numbers gradually became more importantly part of the illusion of bureaucratic control and a key to a colonial imaginary in which countable abstraction, of people and resources at every imaginable level and for every conceivable purpose, created the sense of a controllable indegenous reality" Appadurai, 1996

Heritages in HIS

In limited resource countries, top down HIS have been designed and implemented in two successive waves :
  • Colonial State : statistics as a tool of political and administrative control and justification
  • Developemental State : statistics as a tool of scientific knowledge
"The cornerstones of the experimental culture of development were state spaces where administrators and experts attempted to shape the natural and social environment after their own image, in ways that made them more amenable to their modes of intervention and systems of knowledge." Bonneuil, 2000

Developing bottom-up approaches to HIS
