  • Indicators definition

    • Flexible standard for locally optimized category definitions

    • Hybridization of heterogeneous data sources
    • Actionable map of population for public health actors
  • Data Analysis
    • Using data verification trail
    • Orient supervision of health system

Aim 3 : Data Monitoring

Open Results Based Funding in Bénin

  1. Facilities report monthly on contracted activities
  2. Data is validated by District officers
  3. Payment is made based on validated data

Data

Research question

How to optimize supervision strategy to lower the costs and keep data quality and service quality in check ?

Data Monitoring Questions

  1. How to best screen data quality from routine reports ?
  2. How to best screen service quality from primary health services based on quantitative information ?
  3. What is the optimal amount of supervision we should be making to answer two needs :
    • Global cost reduction
    • Out of Control situations diagnostic

What is in an algorithm ?

  1. A descriptive statistic regarding the distribution of collected indicators
  2. A decision rule to consider the data normal or pathological
  3. An implementation strategy to determine when to supervise facilities

Available Methods

  1. Syndromic surveillance ?
    • Error of predicted value
  2. Health System regulation / benchmarking
    • Inference on posterior distribution of random effects
  3. Industrial Statistical Process Control / Profile Monitoring
    • Monitoring of relationship between indicators

Methods

  1. Fit and calibrate different monitoring models + mixtures
  2. Simulate supervision trails in time with implementation strategies
  3. Design tool for selection of preferred trade-off realisation and corresponding algorithm
